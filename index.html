What is the DIRECTV Stream App?

DIRECTV Stream is a streaming platform from DIRECTV that offers live channels, movies, series, cloud DVR access, and premium network content. Unlike traditional satellite services, DIRECTV Stream works entirely over the internet, and the app allows users to watch content anywhere, anytime.

Where Can You Download the DIRECTV Stream App?

  • Google Play Store (Android)
  • Apple App Store (iPhone/iPad)
  • Amazon Appstore (Fire TV/Firestick)
  • Roku Channel Store
  • Samsung Smart Hub (select TVs)
  • Apple TV Store

How to Download DIRECTV Stream App on Android

  1. Open the Google Play Store on your device.
  2. Search for “DIRECTV Stream”.
  3. Tap on the official app and hit Install.
  4. Once downloaded, open the app and log in using your DIRECTV credentials.

How to Download on iPhone or iPad (iOS)

  1. Open the App Store.
  2. Search for “DIRECTV Stream”.
  3. Tap Get and authenticate with your Apple ID.
  4. Launch the app and sign in.

Download on Roku Devices

  1. Press the Home button on your Roku remote.
  2. Navigate to the Streaming Channels menu.
  3. Search for “DIRECTV Stream”.
  4. Click Add Channel.
  5. Once installed, open the app and log in.

How to Install on Firestick/Fire TV

  1. On the home screen, go to Find > Search.
  2. Type “DIRECTV Stream” and select the app.
  3. Click Download or Get.
  4. After installation, launch the app and sign in.

Using DIRECTV Stream on Smart TVs

Some smart TVs, such as Samsung and LG, support the DIRECTV Stream app via their native app stores. If available, simply search and install the app, then sign in.

Login Requirements

  • Your DIRECTV Stream username and password
  • Stable internet connection
  • Compatible device or operating system

Troubleshooting Download Issues

  • Ensure your device OS is up to date
  • Check storage space before installing
  • Restart your device if the app won't install or open
  • Disable VPNs or ad-blockers if you're facing region errors
